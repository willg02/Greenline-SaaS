# ROADMAP v2

A feature roadmap for Greenline SaaS, inspired by Jobber and tailored for landscaping, plant installation/demo, and garden centers/growers.

## MVP (Minimum Viable Product)
- User authentication & organization management
- Client management (contacts, notes, history)
- Quote/estimate creation and management
- Job scheduling & calendar integration
- Plant/material catalog (inventory, pricing, availability)
- Basic invoicing & payment tracking
- Dashboard with key metrics

## Phase 2: Industry-Specific Enhancements
- Task checklists for jobs (installation, demo, maintenance)
- Route planning & crew assignments
- Plant care guides and recommendations
- Supplier/vendor management
- Purchase orders for materials/plants
- Photo uploads for jobs and plant records

## Phase 3: Advanced Features
- Automated reminders (upcoming jobs, follow-ups, plant care)
- Customer portal (view quotes, invoices, job status)
- Integration with accounting software (e.g., QuickBooks)
- Loyalty programs or discounts for garden centers
- Reporting & analytics (sales, inventory, job performance)
- Mobile app support

## Continuous Improvements
- Feedback system for clients
- Customizable templates (quotes, invoices, emails)
- API for third-party integrations
- Multi-location support for larger businesses
